GoGo Wash Uber-Style On-Demand Car Wash App Connecting Car Washers and Customers

Client Partnership

Guirlene Auguste, an entrepreneur with a vision, partnered with NewAgeSysIT to develop an innovative solution for car owners struggling to maintain their vehicles amidst busy schedules. Recognizing the growing demand for convenient, on-demand car cleaning services, Guirlene conceptualized the GoGo Wash App — an Uber-like on-demand platform connecting car owners with professional washers for prompt and efficient cleaning services.

The Solution

NewAgeSysIT worked closely with Guirlene to design, develop, and launch a user-friendly, feature-rich app. The mission was twofold: to deliver seamless service accessibility for car owners and to create a new income stream for professional washers.

Equipped with real-time request assignments, the on-demand app allows customers to book car cleaning services at their preferred location, significantly reducing wait times and enhancing overall convenience.

Key features

Real-Time Request Assignment

Immediate matching of customers with available washers nearby.

Location-Based Service

Customers can request services wherever they are, whether at home, work or on the go.

Washer Registration

The app allows individuals to register as washers, offering them a flexible source of income.

Effortless Payments

Secure, cashless transactions for easy payment processing.

Feedback System

Customers can rate and review washers, ensuring service quality.

Technology Stack

Node JS
MySQL
CodeIgnitor
AWS

Results

Simplified car maintenance for busy vehicle owners through an intuitive, on-demand solution

Created new economic opportunities for car washers via a flexible gig-based model

Delivered high customer satisfaction with easy accessibility and efficient service delivery

Achieved steady user base growth since launch
