Client Overview & Partnership

Central Florida Transport (CFT), a leading transportation and logistics company, heavily relied on Google Sheets and manual processes involved in quoting, bidding, order management, and reporting. These were not only time-consuming but also error-prone. To resolve these challenges, CFT partnered with NewAgeSysIT to develop a custom ERP and mobility solution. By working closely with the client, we developed a solution that enhanced their workflows, reduced manual effort, and improved responsiveness.

Leveraging Transport & Logistics Operations With A Centralized Automated System

NewAgeSysIT developed a custom ERP and mobility solution for CFT, replacing the manual and cumbersome Google Sheets-based workflows. By adopting an automated yet centralized system, the solution streamlined core processes like bidding, quoting, order management, dispatch tracking, and reporting. We built a mobile application for field and in-house staff to manage jobs on the go. The web-based ERP/CRM helped administrative users to manage all operational data in a single place.

The Solution that Streamlined Transport Operations with Centralized ERP & Mobility System

We developed a mobile app and web-based CRM to streamline the process for in-house and administrative purposes. By optimizing the best use of time and effort, we have enabled cost efficiency. With custom ERP software tailored to meet the specific needs of CFT, we also implement modules for business data, sales management, Fuel, Dispatch, and HR. We have also designed a user-friendly iOS App to complement the ERP system and a user-friendly interface for easy adoption.

Application Overview

We provided CFT a custom development solution that allows the client to tailor the solution to meet the organization's specific requirements. The CFT mobility solution is an iOS-based field and in-house operations app integrated with a custom ERP system. With the solution, the app helped the staff to manage jobs, dispatches, bids, and customer details in real time, eliminating the need for manual spreadsheets.
