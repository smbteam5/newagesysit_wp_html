iOS App Development
Compliance to Check Out

At NewAgeSysIT, we ensure that every iOS application is aligned with privacy, data security, and regulatory compliance standards. Our processes align with U.S. and global frameworks to enable businesses to ensure transparency, trust, and security for the best digital experiences.

HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability & Accountability Act)

Applicable for healthcare applications, the standard ensures that health and patient-specific data are securely stored, encrypted, and accessed only by authorized experts.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

Serves users with control over how their personal data is collected and used, ensuring high transparency for applications running in or serving California users.

COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act)

Applies to applications directed at children under 13, which require parental consent before gathering or sharing personal data.

PCI-DSS

Adopts secure processing and storage of payment-specific information for applications that manage credit/debit card transactions, minimizing security risks.

SOC 2

Ensures that your applications backend systems align with the stringent security, confidentiality, and availability standards, well recognized and accepted by US enterprises.

Apple App Store Guidelines

Includes the mandatory policies for privacy, user security, and performance that every iOS application must comply with before the app launch.

GDPR

Incorporates the international standards to manage personal data, ensuring user consent and transparency for applications that cater to EU users.

ISO/IEC 27001

A globally recognized standard that lays a reliable information security management system (ISMS) to secure sensitive app data.

OWASP Mobile Security Standards

An industrial framework that identifies and alleviates mobile-specific threats, securing apps against regular security threats and data leakages.
