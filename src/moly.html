On-Demand Liquor & Tobacco Delivery App to Digitally Transform Conventional Liquor Business

Client Overview & Partnership

Entrepreneur Sereyvath Chhay partnered with NewAgeSysIT to digitally transform his traditional liquor business. Prior to the pandemic, the operation was entirely offline, relying on in-store visits. When the pandemic disrupted foot traffic and sales, Sereyvath envisioned a mobile and web-based platform to enable online sales and delivery of liquor and tobacco products.He aimed to build a solution that would connect with local retailers, streamline inventory management, and extend his reach to customers seeking the convenience of home delivery.

Refining Liquor & Tobacco Products Delivery with a Secure & Reliable Mobile App

Following in-depth research and close collaboration with project managers, developers, and designers, NewAgeSysIT built a robust e-commerce platform using hybrid technology. The mobile app has helped make placing orders and receiving door delivery of liquor products easier and streamlined.

The Solution That Made Online Liquor Delivery Seamless & Easier

The solution was thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of customers, store owners, and delivery agents, while ensuring full compliance with legal regulations governing liquor sales. The platforms architecture prioritized security, incorporating features to prevent unauthorized access and ensure accurate age verification, creating a safe and reliable digital storefront.

Key Features

Customer Web and Mobile Application:

  • Place orders for liquor products and receive doorstep delivery.
  • Gift products to individuals not using the app.
  • Real-time tracking of delivery agents on the map.
  • Direct communication with stores and delivery agents.
  • Hassle-free order cancellation and refund process.

Store Web Application:

  • Manage inventory and publish products for customers.
  • Process sale orders for timely deliveries.
  • Resolve disputes or cancellations through direct communication.

Delivery Agent Mobile Application:

  • View and search for orders nearby.
  • Ensure correct delivery to verified customers.
  • Coordinate with stores for efficient deliveries.

Our Project Challenges

Regulatory Compliance & Age Verification

Following stringent legal requirements for liquor and tobacco sales demanded secure age verification and location-based restrictions to ensure responsible access.

Multi-User System Architecture

Building a platform that served customers, store owners, and delivery agentseach with unique roles and workflows- demanded a well-structured and scalable system design.

Real-Time Tracking & Communication

Execution of real-time GPS tracking and establishing smooth communication between all users required reliable integrations and a robust backend for fast and accurate updates.
