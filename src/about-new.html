The Story

1994 - 2000 1994 - 2000

The idea to transform industries and businesses digitally began in the early 1993s, eventually leading to the launch of NewAge Systems in 1994. A lot of achievements accompanied the company since its inception until the late 2000s, as follows:

  • Started as a company focusing on web application development and IT services.
  • Ranked #126 in Inc. 500!
  • 9th fastest growing technology company in New Jersey.
2000 - 2005 2000 - 2005

We later decided to change NewAge Systems to NewAgeSys Inc. This timeline was marked by awards, recognitions, and partnerships with reputable clients and business leaders.

  • In New Jersey Fast 50 for 3 Years in a row!
  • NewAgeSys and StatSoft announce offerings for FDA-regulated industries
  • 38th Fastest Growing in PS100 ranking
  • Completes Large SAP Upgrade and Training Project
  • "Preferred" by 7 of the Top 10 pharma/biotech companies
2005 - 2010 2005 - 2010

NewAgeSys achieved numerous successful accomplishments, including recognition from Deloitte, during this period. Our team proposed an idea to expand into mobile applications and new technologies, which became the cornerstone of building the top mobile app development company in the USA.

  • Started working on custom mobile app development services.
  • NJBIZ top 50 fastest-growing companies.
  • Inc. 5000 Americas 500 fastest-growing private companies.
  • Deloitte fastest 50 companies in Greater Philadelphia.
2010 - 2015 2010 - 2015

Later, the time had been invested in growing, evolving, and expanding to new verticals, giving birth to new technologies, innovations, and solutions.

  • Inc.5000 Americas fastest-growing private companies, four years in a row.
  • NJBIZ Fast 50 growing companies.
  • Fast 100 List of USPAACC.
2015 - 2020 2015 - 2020

From there, we introduced the concept of AI and considered giving it life through AI-powered services to revolutionize businesses and the way they operate. We launched a dedicated AI domain to help businesses leverage their AI potential and transform their business workflows.

  • Started working on AI development and consulting services.
  • Fast 100 list of USPAACC consecutively in 2016, 2017, and 2018.
  • Johnson & Johnson service excellence award.
  • NJBIZ Top 25 women-owned businesses
  • Received Commendation from Ortho Clinical Diagnostics.
  • Fast 100 list of USPAACC for the fifth time in 2020.
  • Ranked #135 among the top private companies in New Jersey by NJBIZ
2020 - 2025 2020 - 2025
