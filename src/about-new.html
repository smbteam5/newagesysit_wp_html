The Story

1994 1994 - 2000

NewAgeSys was founded as a staffing and consulting company focused on delivering high-quality technology talent to enterprises in the United States.

  • Established core delivery team in the U.S. and India.
  • Built long-term partnerships with early Fortune 500 clients.
  • Set the foundation for a customer-first culture.
2000 2000 - 2005

During this phase, NewAgeSys strengthened its footprint across the U.S., expanding services from staffing into end-to-end IT solutions.

  • Expanded into new industries and service lines.
  • Scaled delivery teams and established offshore centers.
  • Delivered complex enterprise applications and integrations.
2005 2005 - 2010

NewAgeSys achieved numerous successful accomplishments, including recognition from Deloitte, during this period. Our team proposed an idea to expand into mobile applications and new technologies, which became the cornerstone of building the ‘top mobile app development company’ in the USA.

  • Started working on custom mobile app development services.
  • NJBIZ top 50 fastest-growing companies.
  • Inc. 5000 America’s 500 fastest-growing private companies.
  • Deloitte fastest 50 companies in Greater Philadelphia.
2010 2010 - 2015

With mobile at the core, NewAgeSysIT diversified into cloud, analytics, and enterprise mobility, delivering large-scale transformation programs.

  • Launched dedicated mobility and UX practices.
  • Delivered flagship apps used by millions of users.
  • Deepened relationships with Fortune 500 clients.
2015 2015 - 2020

We evolved into a full-fledged digital product studio, focusing on designing and engineering experiences across web, mobile, and cloud.

  • Expanded portfolio to include AI/ML and IoT projects.
  • Strengthened design-first, product-thinking approach.
  • Recognized across multiple industry platforms.
2020 2020 - 2025

Today, NewAgeSysIT partners with startups and global enterprises to build future-ready, scalable digital products powered by AI and cloud-native architectures.

  • Helping customers modernize legacy platforms.
  • Driving digital transformation across industries.
  • Continuously investing in new technologies and talent.
