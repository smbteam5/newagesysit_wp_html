A standard healthcare app development services company typically takes 3-5 months to develop a healthcare mobile app, whereas a feature-rich platform with EHR/AI integration or telehealth features can take 6-12 months to build and implement.

Apps like MyChart or Teladoc Health are complex and require advanced features to build these enterprise-level capabilities. With advanced features such as real-time video, appointment scheduling, security, and EHR integration, the development costs can range from $150,000 to $500,000+, as per the project's scope, scalability, and depth.
