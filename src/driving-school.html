Internal Messaging System The feature enables real-time communication among students, instructors, and office staff via instant messaging.
Centralized Dashboard A single dashboard to incorporate a unified access to all instructors, students, branches, vehicles, and schedules. Flexible Pricing The option is beneficial for configuring pricing according to the instructor's choice, location, student category, or time of day. Cancellation Monitoring By gaining insights into cancellations, you can identify no-shows and learn patterns to implement rules and reduce operational loss. Compliance & Regulations
We Adhere To

Our custom driving school solutions adhere to U.S. regulatory standards, ensuring high security and compliance. By following advanced regulatory compliance policies, we ensure that the solutions remain protected and relevant to the industry.

Talk to Our Compliance Experts

DMV (Department of Motor Vehicles) Compliance

We develop a DMV-compliant driving school solution that captures mandatory learner data, generates DMV-approved reports, and stores student contracts.

HIPAA Compliance

Our dedicated experts build HIPAA-ready solutions and securely store health-related data (e.g., information on learning disabilities).

PCI-DSS

We develop custom driving school solutions that comply with payment gateway security standards, including integration with Stripe, Square, PayPal, and other platforms.

COPPA Compliance

Our team also ensures that the solutions comply with COPPA, which mandates parental consent protocols for managing data for students under 13.

Data Privacy Regulations

We deliver comprehensive driving school management solutions that comply with data privacy regulations, including CCPA (California) and GDPR (international).
