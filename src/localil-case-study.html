Developing a High-Performing Mobile App to Simplify Social Networking

The client, Ortal Flanary, wanted a mobile application and admin panel solution for Local IL. The platform was designed to connect the Israeli and Jewish communities, and its services focused on enhancing relationships and fostering engagement. They wanted to expand their marketplace and the worldwide network, making social sharing, event management, promotions, and business listings streamlined with a simple yet highly functional mobile app. NewAgeSysIT recognized the need and partnered with the client to deliver a high-performing and user-friendly application, launched on both Android and iOS platforms.

Application Overview

Local IL wanted to develop an app that supports both local business owners and helps people host traditional dinners. By understanding their needs, we created an app with a dedicated space for local business owners to exhibit their products and services, while also supporting the community. We also incorporated a platform that helps to connect with others and cherish the meaningful gatherings.

Features & Functionalities

The mobile application and comprehensive admin panel we developed were designed to deliver an exceptional user experience for administrators and users. The key app functionalities are as follows:

The feature lets users build and manage events, for example, holiday celebrations, community dinner programs.

Businesses can be created and listed within the app, complete with relevant information and media.

Users can share events or business listings with others via integrated sharing options.

Businesses can generate and offer coupon codes to promote discounts and special offers.

A real-time messaging feature allows users to communicate within the app.

Users can view/manage their profiles, update information, and manage account settings.

Admins can review event submissions and choose to approve or deny requests, ensuring the quality and relevance of the content.
