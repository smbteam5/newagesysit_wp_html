Key Advantages of Mobile Apps for Healthcare

01/

Improved Self-Care

mHealth applications make it easy to schedule appointments, check prescriptions, and go through the medical history to ease the workload of healthcare providers.

02/

Healthcare Data Integration

Consolidate data from different sources and get a 360-degree view of patient-related data. The healthcare data integration also helps to achieve great interoperability among the several healthcare systems.

03/

Chronic Healthcare

The devices, when connected to the app, help detect symptoms and measure vitals. The system can be a turning point for patients who seek intensive and consistent healthcare.

04/

Notifications on Medications

Apps send automated alerts to help patients complete their doses, without needing to revisit the hospital. mHealth apps help patients stay on track with their medication schedule.

05/

Virtual App-Led Consultation

Online consultations via an app bring doctors and patients together. It saves travel time, makes healthcare access easier, and also assists users with mobility constraints.

06/

Real-Time Health Monitoring

mHealth apps let users log healthcare data, symptoms, or key recovery milestones. These help clinicians get high-end visibility into the patient health trends, for data-driven solutions and personalized healthcare.

Ensuring Compliance With the HealthTech
Standards & Regulations

As the leading IT healthcare services provider, we focus on developing healthcare applications that adhere to industry standards.
With compliant healthcare solutions, we ensure the protection of patient data and top security of the custom-built applications.

HIPAA

Our team adheres to the highest confidentiality standards, as mandated by HIPAA, with robust access controls and encryption methods, ensuring the security of our healthcare apps.

HL7

We take measures to ensure the apps adhere to the global standards for healthcare information exchange set by HL7, thereby improving the efficiency of healthcare data management.

SOC 2

By guaranteeing the privacy, security, and integrity of healthcare records, we strictly adhere to SOC 2 compliance throughout the application development lifecycle.

FHIR

Through FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) standards, we enhance compatibility and engagement among healthcare organizations for high patient coordination.

HITECH

As the top healthcare application services company, we strictly adhere to HITECH-based security and privacy policies, which enable us to complete all security audits successfully.

FDA

We comply with the strict healthcare standards set by the FDA (Food & Drug Administration), which include documentation, continuous testing, safety, privacy, and software performance regulations.

GDPR

By carefully considering the needs of data collection and management processes to meet GDPR standards, we ensure that the applications maintain the utmost data security and integrity.

PHIPA

We ensure healthcare compliance with PHIPA, or the Personal Health Information Protection Act, which applies to healthcare providers in Ontario, to improve patient data security and confidentiality.
