Quran Easy Quran E-learning 
Application

Project Overview

The client, Iman Khawaja, approached NewAgeSysIT to develop a Quran E-Learning application aimed at providing structured and guided Quran study. His vision was to create a comprehensive solution that seamlessly blends modern technology with traditional teachings, making Quranic learning accessible to Muslim learners across the globe.

The Solution

The project initially began as a web platform development. Based on the client’s needs, our expert team extended the solution to include a mobile app, offering users the flexibility to access curated video content tailored to various aspects of Quranic study. The E-learning application includes the following features:

Application Features

01

User Registration & Verification

The straightforward yet secure registration process makes it simple to access the app's content:

Sign-up

Sign up by entering the name, email address, and password.

Verification of Email

Users receive an email with a verification number, which they must enter to finish the registration process to guarantee account security.

02

Content Categories

The app offers six distinct categories. Each category offers an organized collection of videos, making it easy for users to find and engage with the lessons most relevant to their interests.

Learn to Read the Quran

Surah Recitation

Stories of the Prophets

Daily Reminders

Hadith Studies

Motivational Talks

03

Chapter-Based Lessons

Within each content category, the lessons are divided into chapters to ensure structured learning:

Multiple Video Lessons:

To guarantee a thorough understanding, every chapter includes multiple video lessons that provide a close-up look at the subject.

Track Learning Progress:

After finishing each lesson, users can keep track of their progress, which enables them to pick up where they left off in a chapter.

04

Admin Panel

The admin panel's user-friendly design makes it possible for platform administrators to control user experience and content:

Video management

Administrators can add new videos, thus new content is always being added to the content collection.

Lesson Categorization

Admins have control over assigning videos to specific categories, keeping the content organized and accessible.

User and Content Control

Admins can monitor user activity, manage subscriptions, and oversee the overall functionality of the app.

05

Payment Integration

The app provides multiple payment options to offer flexibility for users:

Stripe Integration

For a seamless payment experience, users can safely make payments with Stripe.

In-App Purchases

This feature increases convenience for iPhone users by allowing payments to be made directly through the app.

06

Subscription Management

The app provides multiple payment options to offer flexibility for users:

Monthly Subscriptions

Quran Easy offers a monthly subscription option to accommodate varying customer choices.

Automatic Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew to ensure uninterrupted access to premium content. Users can cancel their subscription at any time in the settings; however, Apple users must cancel at least one day before the renewal date.
