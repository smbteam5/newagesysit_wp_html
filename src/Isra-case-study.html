ISRA- A Complete App That Quickly Connects Beauty Professionals With The User Base

Client Overview & Partnership

Visionary entrepreneur Iman Khawaja partnered with NewAgeSysIT to streamline the way beauty professionals connect with clients. Inspired by the desire to help barbers, hairstylists, nail technicians, makeup artists, and pet groomers grow their customer base, Iman brought to life ISRAan on-demand service app designed to effortlessly link stylists and clients in one convenient platform.

Building a Successful On-Demand Service App to Connect Stylists & Customers

Iman shared her immense satisfaction by awarding 5-star reviews on platforms like Google and Clutch. The success of ISRA was so impactful that she chose to partner with NewAgeSysIT for a second project, further strengthening the collaboration.

Thanks to this successful partnership, ISRA has become a go-to platform for connecting skilled stylists with customers seeking convenient, high-quality grooming services.

The Solution That Enhanced Stylist Discovery for a Hassle-Free Experience

Iman partnered with NewAgeSysIT to design and develop a user-friendly and dynamic on-demand application. This collaboration has helped the client to build a user-friendly app incorporating features that enable effortless connection of stylists and clients through an all-in-one platform.

Key Features

Stylist Discovery

Users can explore stylists based on ratings, pricing, and distance.

Real-Time Availability

Customers can find and book available stylists in their area at any time.

GPS Tracking

Track stylist locations and estimated arrival times for appointments.

Re-book Feature

Clients can conveniently re-book their preferred stylists.

Diverse Service Range

Includes personal grooming services and pet grooming for a broader audience.

Secure Transactions:

Integration of secure payment gateways for seamless transactions.
