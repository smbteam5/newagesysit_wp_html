Regular User version

  • Is it safe to host or join a dinner? expand_more

    Yes. All users are verified, and your exact address is never public. It’s shared only after you approve a guest.

  • Can everyone in the app see my address? expand_more

    No. Your exact address is never visible to the public. It only appears after you approve someone to join your dinner or event.

  • How is my privacy protected on LOCAL-IL? expand_more

    Your data and personal details are encrypted and securely stored on AWS servers. We do not share your information with any third parties.

  • Can I decline someone who asks to join my dinner? expand_more

    Yes. Every host has complete control to accept or decline any participant — no explanation needed.

  • Can I use LOCAL-IL anywhere, even while traveling? expand_more

    Absolutely! You can use LOCAL-IL wherever you are. Most users are currently in the United States, but we aim to make the app global and connect Jewish communities worldwide.

  • Can every business owner advertise on LOCAL-IL? expand_more

    Yes! Any Jewish business owner can promote their business on LOCAL-IL and reach the community.

  • Is it free to advertise my business? expand_more

    Yes. During the launch phase, all business listings are completely free — no credit card required.

  • How do I advertise my business on LOCAL-IL? expand_more

    It's easy! On the home screen, tap “Business Services,” then “Create Your Business.” It takes less than two minutes to set up your profile.

  • How can I contact support if I need help? expand_more

    You can contact us anytime through the Contact Us section in the app, or by emailing support@local-il.com.

  • How can Jewish organizations collaborate with LOCAL-IL? expand_more

    We welcome partnerships! Contact us via the Contact Us section in the app or email support@local-il.com to discuss collaboration opportunities and receive your free organization user access.
