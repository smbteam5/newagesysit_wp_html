This feature gives the consumers the option to schedule rides by indicating the pickup and drop-off locations and the mode of transportation (e.g., bike, carpool, taxi).

Real-Time Vehicles Tracking

Utilize GPS technology to give customers real-time updates on the whereabouts of reserved cars so they may monitor their progress.

Reliability Provide consumers with an estimated trip cost by calculating prices based on factors such as vehicle type, surge pricing, distance traveled, and additional costs.

Various Methods of Payment

Enable users to purchase premium content, subscriptions, or merchandise directly through the app for convenience and revenue generation.

Reviews and Rating

Allow users to evaluate and comment on drivers and cars to uphold service standards and foster community trust.

Push Alerts

Keep users informed and engaged with timely updates on new content releases, events, or personalized recommendations.

Driver Overviews

To assist consumers in making wise judgments, display driver information such as ratings, reviews, and vehicle specifications.

Chat/In-App Messaging

Assist users and drivers in communicating about trip specifics, instructions for pickup, and other questions.

Travel Record

For convenience, keep track of previous rides' locations for pickup and drop-off, rates, driver information, and invoices.

Advance Reservation

Give people the option to reserve rides ahead of time so they can arrange their transportation with a verified reservation.

Multilingual Assistance

Provide a variety of language choices to cater to a varied user base and make sure the app is simple and accessible for individuals with different language tastes.

Journey Collaboration

For enhanced safety, allow users to communicate journey information with friends and family, including the anticipated time of arrival.

Getting Around and Scheduling

Provide precise instructions, the best routes, and real-time traffic updates for effective mobility by integrating with mapping services.
