Sustainability

Foogly

Turning Food Waste into a Sustainable Future

Problem Statement

Millions of tons of food go to waste every year due to the lack of a direct farm-to-consumer connection.

About the Project

Kevin Speina, PhD in Chemistry with a specialization in Green Chemistry, envisioned a sustainable way to reduce global food waste. What started as a research concept soon evolved into a viable platform. Kevin partnered with NewAgeSysIT to transform his vision into Foogly — a digital marketplace that connects farmers directly with consumers.

We built a scalable, intuitive e-commerce system that empowers farmers to sell surplus produce, promote sustainability, and reduce environmental impact.

Testimonial

"Foogly turned my academic research into a real, working solution. The NewAgeSysIT team didn’t just build an app — they built impact."

Dr. Kevin Speina, Founder of Foogly

Auntie App

Empowering Women through Trusted Childcare

Problem Statement

Working mothers often struggle to find reliable, verified babysitters — balancing career and family becomes a daily challenge.

About the Project

Founded by two working professionals who understood this pain firsthand, Auntie App was built to make childcare safe, transparent, and empowering.

NewAgeSysIT collaborated to design and develop a secure, AI-driven caregiver platform that verifies babysitters, manages bookings, and ensures care transparency — empowering women to work confidently while ensuring their children are in safe hands.

Testimonial

“We wanted to build more than an app — we wanted to build trust. NewAgeSysIT made that vision possible.”

Founder, Auntie App

HIPAA-driven Transformation

CashDocs

Transforming Doctor-Patient Communication

Problem Statement

Patients and healthcare providers struggle with inefficient appointment systems and fragmented communication.

About the Project

When a physician and a legal expert came together, they envisioned a digital bridge between doctors and patients that’s efficient, compliant, and easy to use.

NewAgeSysIT built CashDocs, a HIPAA-compliant healthcare platform that simplifies scheduling, enables secure patient communication, and reduces administrative friction. From concept to compliance, we designed CashDocs to deliver accessibility and trust — one appointment at a time.

Testimonial

“Our goal was to simplify healthcare without compromising security. NewAgeSysIT turned that dream into a seamless experience.”

Dr. A. Williams, Co-founder of CashDocs

Cross-Cultural Learning - EdTech

Quran Easy

Building Bridges Through Learning

Problem Statement

Learners worldwide seek accessible, structured ways to study and understand the Quran in a modern, inclusive format.

About the Project

Quran Easy began as a vision to make faith-based education more accessible globally. The founder approached NewAgeSysIT to design a digital learning experience where users could learn at their own pace, with structured lessons and multilingual support.

We developed an eLearning platform with adaptive learning paths, multimedia modules, and cloud-based progress tracking — designed to promote inclusivity, cultural respect, and global access to knowledge.

Testimonial
