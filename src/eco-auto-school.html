Eco Auto School A Driving School Platform To Support Students And Instructors

Client Overview

ECO AUTO SCHOOL, led by Ramon Marte in New York, is a driving school committed to delivering comprehensive, high-quality driving education. Recognizing the limitations of their existing third-party SaaS software, they turned to NewAgeSysIT to develop a customized solution that could better support both their students and instructors.

The Challenge

The primary challenges faced included

Limited Customization
The existing SaaS platform was unable to meet specific operational needs.

Inefficient Scheduling
Manual scheduling processes led to conflicts and operational inefficiencies.

Lack of Real-Time Tracking
The inability to track driving sessions in real time impacted the quality and oversight of instruction.

High Administrative Overhead
Managing payments, instructor availability, and student progress was cumbersome and time-consuming.

The Solution

Our team collaborated closely with ECO AUTO SCHOOL to gain a deep understanding of their operations, challenges, and goals. The objective was clear: streamline driving school management for students, instructors, and administrators through a comprehensive mobile solution.

NewAgeSysIT developed a bespoke mobile application tailored specifically to ECO AUTO SCHOOL's unique requirements. The solution encompassed:

01

Student Portal
Enables students to register, purchase courses, confirm appointments, and track session progress, providing full control over their learning journey.

02

Instructor Module
Equips instructors with tools to view schedules, manage sessions, receive real-time notifications, track student status (confirmed, pending, completed), and initiate sessions directly from the app.

03

Admin Dashboard
Provides administrators with full access to manage users, monitor session statuses, oversee scheduling, and process payments, ensuring smooth and efficient backend operations.

04

Real-Time Session Monitoring
Enables live tracking of instructor sessions, enhancing safety, accountability, and operational transparency.

05

User-Friendly Navigation
A well-organized side menu allows users to easily access dashboards, schedules, notifications, privacy settings, and more.

Key Features

    Key Features of the Student Module

  • Registration & Course Purchase
    Students can easily sign up and purchase courses, including the 5-hour class, directly through the app.

  • Learner's Permit Upload
    Upload required documents, such as learners permits, during registration to ensure a smooth onboarding process.

  • Dashboard Access
    Personalized greetings with quick links to key features like driving packages, instructional videos, learning guides, and test preparation materials.

  • Appointment Scheduling
    Book lessons based on ZIP code, preferred time slots, and instructor availability using a calendar-based interface.

  • Instructor Selection
    Browse and select instructors by profile pictures, ratings, availability, and personal preferences for a customized experience.

  • Flexible Scheduling
    Choose time slots with detailed instructor notes, ensuring personalized lesson planning.

  • Driving Test Requests
    Submit forms to request and schedule road tests seamlessly through the app.

  • Progress Tracking
    Visual indicators and logs allow students to track milestones and monitor readiness for driving tests.

  • Permit Test Preparation
    Access structured learning modules for Car, Motorcycle, and CDL permits to prepare effectively.

  • Student Services
    Provides driving guides, practice tests (for Car, Motorcycle, and CDL), and tools to log home driving experience.

  • Driving Videos & Training Guides
    A rich library of video content and tutorials tailored for both teen and adult learners.

  • Tutorial Access
    Quick access to practice materials, learning guides, and tutorials anytime, anywhere.

  • Support Tickets
    Students can raise concerns or queries directly within the app and receive timely assistance.

  • Gift Packages
    Purchase and send driving lesson packages to friends or family as thoughtful gifts.

    Key Features of the Instructor Module

  • Dashboard Overview
    • Displays total upcoming schedules
    • Shows counts for assigned students and completed sessions
    • Offers a quick snapshot of instructor activity
  • Availability Management

    Instructors can set and update their available time slots for sessions based on their preferences and location.

  • Schedule Management
    • View all upcoming appointments with session time, date, and location
    • Session status clearly labeled (Confirmed, Pending, Completed)
    • Instructors can start sessions directly from the dashboard
  • Student Info Access
    • Easily view student name, session code, and number of participants
    • One-tap option to call the student directly for coordination
  • Live Tracking

    Real-time session monitoring ensures accountability and safety during each driving session.

  • Progress Updates

    Instructors can provide notes and feedback to track the students performance and learning curve.

  • Notifications Center
    • Receive real-time alerts when a student confirms a session
    • View all past notifications with clear timestamps
  • Profile Section
    • Displays instructor photo and personalized greeting
    • Quick link to view and update profile details
  • Menu Navigation

    Easy access to dashboard, schedules, notifications, profile, privacy settings, and logout options

    Admin Panel Features

  • User Management

    Add or remove students and instructors.

  • Role Assignment

    Create sub-admins with specific permissions.

  • Payment Processing

    Manage weekly payouts and view financial reports.

  • Session Oversight

    Monitor all scheduled appointments and sessions.

  • Live Tracking

    Access real-time tracking data for ongoing sessions.

Results

The implementation of the custom-built mobile app led to measurable improvements across all areas of ECO AUTO SCHOOLs operations

1600+ Active Learners Onboarded: The app attracted over 1600 active users, significantly expanding the school's student base.

75% Reduction in Scheduling Errors: Automated scheduling minimized manual mismatches and eliminated double bookings.

100% Adoption of Real-Time Tracking: All instructors now utilize GPS tracking, enhancing session transparency and safety.

2x Faster Onboarding: New student registrations and course purchases became twice as fast through the apps streamlined registration and payment process.
