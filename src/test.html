Simple Steps

Step 01

Requirement Collection

Share your IT team augmentation requirements with us, which include skills, experience levels, project background, and duration.

Step 02

Candidate Selection & Rate Card Presentation

We evaluate your requirements, pull candidates from our talent pool, and present you with profiles and their rate cards.

Step 03

Candidate Interviews

After the previous step, you can shortlist candidates and conduct interviews with them to pick the right fit for your project.

Step 04

Contract Signing

Once you select the candidate(s), we start formalizing contract terms, like pricing, engagement model, IP, etc.

Step 05

Onboarding & Integration

The selected professional(s) are then onboarded and integrated into your team/workflow so your work can begin smoothly.
